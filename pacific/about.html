Our Story & Values

Founded in 2010, Pacific Trails Resort was born out of a passion for the rugged beauty of the North Coast. Our goal is to provide a serene escape where guests can reconnect with nature without sacrificing luxury. Whether you are here for the world-class hiking or a quiet yoga retreat, our team is dedicated to your peace of mind.

Our team of expert guides is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and unforgettable experience for every adventurer who joins our resort family.
